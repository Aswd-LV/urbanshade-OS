Account Security Tips

  • Use a strong, unique password that you don't use elsewhere
  • Never share your password with anyone - staff will never ask for it
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Review your privacy settings in Account Settings
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts claiming to be from Urbanshade
  • Log out on shared or public computers
  • Regularly review your active sessions

Reporting Issues

If you encounter harassment, abuse, or rule violations, please report it immediately. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action.

What to Report

  • Harassment or bullying in chat or messages
  • Spam or advertising
  • Impersonation of other users or staff
  • Sharing inappropriate content
  • Cheating or exploiting bugs
  • Any behavior that makes you uncomfortable

  • Message privacy - control who can send you messages
