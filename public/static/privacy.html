Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Urbanshade OS collects, uses, and protects your information.

Summary: We collect minimal data, store most things locally in your browser, and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Local Storage

Most data (settings, files, preferences) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device unless you create an account for cloud sync.

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username (display name)
  • Password (securely hashed)
  • Profile information you choose to add

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the service, such as:

  • Feature usage frequency
  • Error reports
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To sync your data across devices (if enabled)
  • To enable social features (chat, messaging)
  • To improve and optimize the experience
  • To communicate service updates
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Provide data to third parties for marketing

We may share data:

  • With service providers necessary for operation (hosting, database)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect rights and safety

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that follow you across the web.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to user data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

To exercise these rights, visit Account Settings or contact us through our support page.

7. Children's Privacy

Urbanshade OS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our support page.
